Privacy Policy

About

Reppy is developed and operated by Benjamin Pozin, based in Canada. By using this app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Your data stays on your device.

Reppy stores all workout data, templates, splits, and preferences locally on your device. We have no servers, no user accounts, and collect no information remotely.

What's stored locally

• Workouts you log (exercises, sets, weights, reps)
• Templates and training splits you create
• App preferences (weight units, theme, rest timer, notification time)

Permissions

Reppy may request the following permissions. Both are optional and the app works without them.

• Apple Health: to save completed workouts to the Health app
• Notifications: to send daily workout reminders at a time you choose

Neither permission is used for advertising, tracking, or any purpose beyond core app functionality.

Apple Health

If you grant permission, Reppy writes completed workout sessions to Apple Health as strength training activities. Reppy does not read any Health data. Health data is never used for advertising, data mining, or shared with third parties. Reppy complies with Apple's HealthKit guidelines.

Third parties

Reppy contains no analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs. No data is sold, rented, or shared with any third party.

Deleting your data

All data lives only on your device. Deleting the app permanently removes everything. There is no account and no server-side data to request removal of.

Children's privacy

Reppy is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Applicable law

This policy is governed by the laws of Ontario, Canada. We handle any personal information in accordance with applicable privacy legislation, including PIPEDA.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the date at the bottom. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
