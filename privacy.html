Privacy Policy

This policy describes how Reppy (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) handles information when you use the Reppy mobile application. We keep this clear and practical.

Information we collect

Reppy is designed to minimize data collection. In general:

  • Workout and app content you create: exercises, sets, reps, weights, and related data you enter are stored to provide the app’s features (logging, stats, achievements, pet progress, etc.).
  • Device and diagnostics: if you contact support or if the app uses standard platform analytics or crash reporting permitted by Apple, we may receive limited technical information (for example, app version, device type, or crash logs) to fix bugs and improve stability. We do not use this to sell advertising profiles.
  • Communications: if you email us (for example, at our support address), we receive your email address and the content of your message.

We do not ask you to create an account on this website, and this support site does not require you to submit personal data to view it.

How we use information

We use information only to operate and improve Reppy: to show your workouts and stats, sync or store data as the app is designed to do, respond to support requests, fix crashes, and understand broad usage so the product works better. We do not sell your personal data.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only when necessary to run the service (for example, with cloud or infrastructure providers that host app data under strict terms), or when required by law. Any such providers are chosen to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Data security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information appropriate to the nature of the app. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure; we work to reduce risk and address issues when reported.

Children’s privacy

Reppy is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will take steps to delete it where appropriate.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when Reppy or the law changes. We will post the new version on this page and update the “Last updated” date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy, unless a law requires a different process.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email reppysupport@gmail.com.
